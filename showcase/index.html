Methodology

I
Two-stage label discovery

40% sample → Gemini Pro discovers candidate topics/outcomes. Remaining 60% classified by Gemini Flash against fixed schema to prevent naming drift.

II
Raw form preservation

No stopword removal, lemmatization, or stemming. Conversational cues, hesitations, and agent phrasing carry signal — stripping them degrades classification.

III
Hybrid retrieval decision

Graph strategy: single-concept expansion, multi-concept intersection, shortest-path bridging. Vector strategy: must-match domain + should-match topic/outcome.

IV
Faithfulness as guardrail

GraphRAG's 0.74 faithfulness means near-zero hallucination risk. Critical for compliance and root-cause analysis where incorrect citations are dangerous.
